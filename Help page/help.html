WEBBIE DESCRIPTION

Webbie is an interactive tutorial for beginners wanting to learn web development. It focuses on HTML and CSS.

ABOUT

After joining TAP at GGC, our group was formed and we agreed on creating an informational game about web development that was fun for and simple enough for younger students like middle schoolers. After 2 months of trail, error, suggestions, and new ideas, we came up with our current version of webbie.
