ABOUT

PROJECT- WEBBIE

After joining TAP(Technology Ambassador program) at GGC(Gerogia Gwinnet College), our group was formed and we agreed on creating an informational game about web development that was fun for and simple enough for younger students such as middle schoolers. After 2 months of trail, error, suggestions, and new ideas, we came up with our current version of webbie. Our game is still in progress and we hope to add more activities and customizations! Thank you for taking an interest in our project and please take our survey.

ABOUT

TAP - Technology Ambassdor Program

Technology Ambassador Program(TAP) is a program, at GGC(Gerogia Gwinnet College), where students interested in technology work together to create a project and work on numerous skills and career development during the process. The project is later showcased in several events.
